  • JALLOSH

- Established in 1964, Cosmopolitan Education Society is a premier educational institution catering to the needs of more than 7,000 students from nursery to post-graduation level.

- The Valia College of Commerce, Arts, and Science takes pride in organizing COSMOS JALLOSH, our annual inter-collegiate cultural fest to provide a platform for students across the city to explore their creative skills.

- Our festival is called JALLOSH meaning “Celebrating with the community”. We at Valia College believe that each member of the student community who participates in COSMOS JALLOSH has the seal of the Eagle, the ability to prove oneself and therefore the eagle represents the undying spirit and resilience of the youth. Thus, it is a festival that nurtures and celebrates the spirit of youth.
